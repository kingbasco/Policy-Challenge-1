about our Policy Challenge

What is the Policy Challenge?

The Policy Challenge is a national competition that brings together students, researchers, and early-career professionals to develop practical, research-based solutions to Nigeria’s most pressing governance and development challenges. Participants submit policy proposals, and the most promising ideas are presented at the annual Policy Conference.

2026 Theme

Rebuilding Trust in the Nigerian State: Institutional Reforms for Legitimacy, Effectiveness, and Inclusive Governance

The 2026 edition invites participants to explore practical reforms that can strengthen public institutions, improve accountability, and restore citizens’ confidence in governance. Proposals should focus on bold, implementable ideas that make institutions more transparent, effective, and responsive to the people they serve.
